Uniqueness

The upgrades actually change how the game feels, not like those lazy '+1 damage' upgrades that literally every other clicker just throws at you.

Instead of static screens, the visual horizon keeps expanding as you play. New areas and mechanics unlock naturally, so progress is something you actually see, not just a number going up.

Progress continues while you’re away, and when you return, it’s immediately clear how far you’ve come and what changed during your absence.
